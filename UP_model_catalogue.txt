Here are the most important parameters / directories that help navigate the SP / UP model:


IC input files, output frequency and namelist:							/models/atm/cam/bld/user_nl_cam
Choose UM5 or MPDATA advection scheme:								/models/atm/cam/bld/configure
Define compsets / compset related arguments:							/scripts/ccsm_utils/Case.template/config_compsets.xml
Level of CRM acceleration is hardwired at:							/models/atm/cam/src/physics/cam/phys_control.F90 
-DCRMACCEL ifdef flag (line 146, L256 on Cori) hardwired at:					/scripts/ccsm_utils/Machines/config_compilers.xml
Directory of “use_cases”									/models/atm/cam/bld/namelist_files/use_cases/
-r8 double precision compiler option								add a “<ADD_FFLAG> -r8 …” line after –DCRM line in /scripts/ccsm_utils/Machines/config_compilers.xml
Aerosol concentration scale factor								/models/atm/cam/src/physics/cam/microp_aero.F90
Template machine dependent files on Cori							/global/project/projectdirs/ccsm1/collections/cesm1_2_2/scripts/ccsm_utils/Machines
